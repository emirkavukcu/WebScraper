CARICOM 50th Anniversary Logo Competition

July 4th 2023, will mark the 50th anniversary of the Caribbean Community (CARICOM).

To commemorate this milestone, the Caribbean Community Secretariat(CCS) is inviting entries from nationals of the community for the fiftieth anniversary logo bases on the theme: "50 Years Strong: A Solid Foundation to Build On".
