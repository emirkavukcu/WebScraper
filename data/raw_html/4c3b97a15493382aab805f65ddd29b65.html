Recruitment of Vincentian Nurses in the United Kingdom

Details
Published: 09 May 2018

The High Commission of Saint Vincent and Grenadines in the United Kingdom, through the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Trade and Commerce invites applications for the employment of trained Vincentian nurses who are currently unemployed.

The recruitment process runs from Friday 4th to Thursday 10th May, 2018. To be eligible for selection, prospective beneficiaries should meet the following criteria:

  • Be a citizen of Saint Vincent and the Grenadines;
  • Hold a minimum of a Bachelor’s Degree in Nursing;
  • Be in good health;
  • Provide an up-to-date Curriculum Vitae (CV); and
  • Complete the Global Talent Bank Form online or at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Trade and Commerce.

All supporting documentation must be fully completed to be considered in the first round of selection. All available information is provided at http://foreign.gov.vc/foreign/ and all applications should be submitted to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Trade and Commerce by 4:00 p.m. on Thursday 10th, May.

The Ministry remains committed to enabling increased human and social development for every Vincentian.


For further information, please contact: Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Trade and Commerce at 784-456-2060.
